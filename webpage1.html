the Walk

The walk from Monterosso to Riomaggiore will take you approimately two hours, give or take an hours depending on the weather condition and the physical shape.

What ??

Cinque Terre compries five Village:Monterosso, vernazza,Cornilia,Manarla,And Riomaffiore.

Where?

On the northwest cost of the Italian Riviera, north of the city La Spezia.

Price?

The Walk is free!
